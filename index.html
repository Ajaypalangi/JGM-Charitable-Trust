About Us

Who We Are

A registered charitable trust with a mission to uplift underprivileged communities

Since 2023

JGM Charitable Trust is a legally registered trust established in October 2023 under the leadership of Kola Yesu, Founder President, with the vision of creating a just, equitable, and compassionate society.

Based in Tadivaripalle Village, Tharlupadu Mandal, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh, we work tirelessly to provide opportunities for women, children, and marginalized communities through skill development, healthcare, education, and welfare programs.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities by providing access to education, healthcare, livelihood skills, and social welfare — building a self-reliant and dignified life for all.

Our Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of background, has equal opportunity to thrive — free from poverty, ignorance, and inequality.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Inclusion, and Accountability guide every initiative we undertake. We believe in transparent operations and community-first service.

Our Care Home

Old Age Home & Orphan Children Home

A safe haven providing love, care, healthcare, and education in a family-like environment

JGM Charitable Trust proudly operates an integrated Old Age Home and Orphan Children Home. We believe that bringing together the wisdom of the elderly and the vibrant energy of children creates a beautiful synergy that benefits both generations. We provide shelter, highly nutritious food, healthcare, and equal opportunities for everyone.

Dual Purpose Construction

This new building project is designed to integrate two essential community needs into one campus, promoting intergenerational synergy:

  • Old Age Home: A safe, warm, and comfortable shelter for the elderly, providing round-the-clock care, nutritional food, and health monitoring.
  • School (Nursery to 10th Class): High-quality, modern educational classrooms dedicated to offering free and affordable schooling for local children.
